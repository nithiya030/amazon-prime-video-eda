# Amazon Prime Video EDA

## Project Overview
This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the Amazon Prime Video dataset to understand content trends, genres, ratings, production countries, and audience preferences.

## Objective
- Analyze content distribution across Movies and TV Shows.
- Identify popular genres and production countries.
- Explore relationships between ratings, popularity, and runtime.
- Provide business insights and recommendations based on the analysis.

## Tools & Libraries
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn

## Dataset
- titles.csv
- credits.csv

## Key Insights
- Movies dominate the Amazon Prime library.
- TV Shows have higher average IMDb ratings than Movies.
- Drama is the most common genre.
- History and Documentation have the highest average IMDb ratings.
- Most content is produced by a few major countries.
- IMDb and TMDB ratings show a moderate positive correlation.

## Business Recommendations
- Increase investment in high-quality TV Shows.
- Expand content from underrepresented countries.
- Improve the quality of popular genres such as Drama and Comedy.
- Continue investing in successful regional content.
